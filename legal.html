My Rights to Speak the Truth

I am Fariz, from Indonesia. This page exists to explain what protects me legally as I expose unsafe platforms.

Legal Protections

Freedom of Expression

According to Indonesia's constitution (Article 28E) and global human rights law (UDHR Article 19), I have the right to express my experiences and concerns.

Protected Whistleblowing

Reporting unsafe digital environments is not defamation. It's public interest speech. I show facts, not hate.

No Harm, No Violation

This website does not leak private data, threaten users, or attack companies. I highlight moderation failures and unsafe patterns. That is allowed.
