Roblox: Not As Safe As It Seems

I’ve met two predators on Roblox — one from Canada and one from my country, Indonesia. Roblox says it’s for kids, but its moderation tells another story.

What I've Witnessed

Predator Encounters

They approached in-game and used private chats. I recognized red flags and blocked them — but they were never removed from the platform.

Moderation Failure

I reported them. Nothing happened. Roblox’s auto-mod just filters random words, not real danger. Reports don’t get real action.

False Sense of Safety

Parents think it’s safe. But behind avatars and games, predators know how to stay hidden and target kids easily.
