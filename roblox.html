Roblox: Not As Safe As It Seems

I’ve met two predators on Roblox — one from Canada and one from my own country, Indonesia. Roblox says it’s for kids, but its moderation tells another story.

What I've Witnessed

Predator Encounters

I was approached by strangers in private messages pretending to be kids. I blocked them, but Roblox did nothing — they stayed online, free to talk to others.

Moderation Failure

Even after reporting with evidence, moderation ignored it. Their filter blocks words, not people. Dangerous users slip through easily.

Exploits & Unsafe Games

I've seen games that bypass filters with inappropriate content. Some mimic popular games to trick kids. These stay online for weeks.

False Sense of Security

Roblox advertises as safe for kids, but in reality, danger hides in plain sight — from chatrooms to sponsored games with poor oversight.

Neglected Reports

Reports take days, often never reviewed. I’ve tested this by reporting obvious abuse. No response. It’s like shouting into the void.
